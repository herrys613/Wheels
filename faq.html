We’re happy to help!

Check out the answers to some frequently asked questions below. Got more questions? Contact us anytime! —we’re happy to help with anything you need!

What is Driveway?
Driveway is a platform that brings together the best car leasing deals from top leasing companies. We make it easy for you to find the perfect car at a great price, all in one place.
How does Driveway work?
Simply browse our website to find the best car leasing deals. Once you find a car you’re interested in, you can apply for the lease directly through our platform. We earn a commission when a lease is finalized.
Is there a fee for using Driveway?
No, there is absolutely no fee for using Driveway. All of our services are completely free to car leasers. We earn a commission from the leasing companies when you complete a car lease through our platform.
Can I return the car before the lease term ends?
Some leasing companies may allow early termination of your lease, but this often comes with additional fees. It’s important to check the terms of your specific lease agreement for any early termination clauses.
What happens if I exceed my mileage limit on a car lease?
Exceeding your mileage limit may result in additional fees. Be sure to review the terms of your lease to understand the costs associated with exceeding the mileage allowance.
How do I know if I qualify for a car lease?
Qualifications vary by leasing company, but generally, you’ll need a stable income, good credit, and a clean driving record. Contact us for specific eligibility requirements.
Do I need insurance for a leased car?
Yes, most leasing companies require full coverage insurance for the duration of the lease. Check with your leasing company for their insurance requirements.
